Advancement of symbolic logic as a mathematical discipline with application of logical statements .

Logic

  • Logic is known as the science of reasoning.
  • It is critical in effective communication, construction of valid arguments, and making decisions.

Statements

Simple Statement

Conveys a single idea or thought.

Compound Statement

Delivers two or more ideas or thoughts.

Logic Connectives and Symbols

Statement Connective Symbol Type
not p not ~p Negation
p and q and p ∧ q Conjunction
p or q or p V q Disjunction
If p, then q If...then... p → q Conditional
p if and only if q If and only if p ↔ q Biconditional

Example

Given: Let p = "It is raining" and q = "I bring an umbrella"

1. What is ~p?

~p = "It is not raining"

2. What is p ∧ q?

p ∧ q = "It is raining and I bring an umbrella"

3. What is p → q?

p → q = "If it is raining, then I bring an umbrella"
